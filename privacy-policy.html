Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-16

This Privacy Policy explains how NexaLife ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the NexaLife mobile application and related services.

1. Data we collect

  • Account information (such as email address and user ID).
  • Profile and app usage data that you provide in the app.
  • Technical data (device, app version, log information).

2. How we use data

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To provide app features and personalize the user experience.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve performance.
  • To communicate important service notices.

3. Legal basis

We process personal data based on contract performance, legitimate interests, and where required by law, user consent.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by trusted service providers (for example cloud hosting, authentication, and analytics) only as needed to operate the service.

5. Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. You may also have the right to data portability and to withdraw consent.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal data. No method of storage or transfer is fully secure.

8. Children

NexaLife is not intended for children under the minimum age permitted by applicable law in your country.

9. Contact

For privacy requests, contact: info@nexalife.fi

For incident situations, contact: support@nexalife.fi
